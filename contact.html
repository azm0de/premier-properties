Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and process

What sets Premier Properties apart from other luxury real estate firms?

+

Our unique combination of local market expertise, global network access, and personalized white-glove service ensures exceptional results. We provide exclusive access to off-market properties and maintain relationships with the world's most discerning buyers and sellers.

Do you work with international clients?

+

Absolutely. We have extensive experience with international transactions and maintain partnerships with luxury real estate firms worldwide. Our team can navigate cross-border transactions, currency considerations, and legal requirements.

What is your typical transaction timeline?

+

Timeline varies based on property type and market conditions. Luxury transactions typically take 30-90 days from offer acceptance to closing. We work efficiently while ensuring every detail is properly managed for a smooth transaction.

Do you offer property management services?

+

We provide concierge services and can coordinate with trusted property management partners. Our network includes vetted professionals for maintenance, security, landscaping, and other property care needs.

How do you ensure client privacy and discretion?

+

Client confidentiality is paramount. We have strict privacy protocols, offer off-market property searches, and can facilitate anonymous transactions when required. All team members sign comprehensive confidentiality agreements.
